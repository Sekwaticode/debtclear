Our Solutions

Debt Relief Services

At Clear Debt, we specialize in helping South Africans legally and effectively clear their debt records. From removing outdated or unlawful listings to providing expert credit advice, our goal is to restore your financial freedom and empower you to move forward with confidence.

Debt Review Removal

We help you legally exit debt review once your financial situation has improved, restoring your credit status and financial independence.

Prescribed Debt Removal

If your debt has prescribed according to South African law, we assist in removing it from your credit profile so you’re not unfairly pursued.

Credit Bureau Update

We work with the credit bureaus to update and correct your record, ensuring that outdated or inaccurate information is cleared promptly.

Judgment Removal

Our team assists in removing court judgments that have been settled or should no longer appear on your record, improving your creditworthiness.

Credit Advice

We provide expert guidance on how to manage your credit, rebuild your score, and make informed financial decisions for the future.

Admin Order Removal

We assist in lifting outdated or unnecessary administration orders so you can regain full financial control and freedom.

The Process

What to expect on your journey with Clear Debt

Step 1: Free Credit Check

We start by offering you a free credit report check. This helps us understand your financial position and identify areas where we can assist.

Step 2: Debt & Financial Analysis

Our team carefully analyzes your debts and financial standing, looking at your obligations, income, and overall credit health.

Step 3: Tailored Assistance Plan

Once we have the full picture, we inform you of the best solutions and services we can provide to help you reduce or remove your debts.

Step 4: Documentation & Quotation

We then send you all the necessary documents along with a clear quotation, so you know exactly how the process will work and what to expect.

Step 5: Secure Debit Order Setup

Finally, we send you a debit order for your approval, ensuring a simple and secure way to manage your payments with us.
